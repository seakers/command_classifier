Satellite Bus
Satellite is an integrated bus and payload system
Bus is the infrastructure of the satellite
Bus performs a multitude of functions
Provides the frame
Maintains temperature
Provides power
Orients the payload
Reaches and maintains orbit
Transmits mission and tracking data to ground
Maintains control of all satellite systems
Each subsystem performs a unique function, but its designs are dependent on all other systems and the mission payload
