Remote Sensor types
Visible Systems
Sensors that operate from the ultraviolet to the red end of the visual spectrum, limited to daylight operation
Operate in various bands throughout the infrared spectrum
Infrared Systems
Radar Systems
Microwave Radiometers
Operate in the centimeter to millimeter bands and are active instruments that provide their own illumination of the scene
Operate in the 20 200GHz range, worse resolution than visible but can collect data over wide range
